The Importance of Semantic HTML

By Alex Carter | July 22, 2025

Why Semantic HTML Matters

Semantic HTML improves accessibility, SEO, and maintainability. It helps search engines and screen readers understand your content better.

Tags like <article>, <section>, and <time> give meaning to the content beyond its appearance.

Common Semantic Tags

  • <header> – Page or section header
  • <nav> – Navigation links
  • <article> – Independent content
  • <section> – Thematic grouping
  • <footer> – Footer content

Conclusion

Writing semantic HTML isn't just good practice—it’s essential. It ensures your websites are more accessible and easier to maintain over time.
